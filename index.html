Understanding Quality of Service Classes in Mobile Networks

What is QCI?

QoS Class Identifier (QCI) is a mechanism used in LTE networks to ensure proper Quality of Service for different types of traffic. Each QCI has specific characteristics that determine how network resources are allocated.
